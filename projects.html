Projects

Project 1: Traffic accident detection

The objectives of this project include:
a. Developing an accident detection system using YOLO for real-time analysis of video sequences.
b. Training the YOLO model on a comprehensive dataset of labelled video frames to enable accurate accident detection.
c. Evaluating the performance of the system in terms of accuracy, precision, recall, and response time.
d. Integrating the system with notification mechanisms to alert relevant authorities promptly.
e. Assessing the potential impact of the system on reducing accident severity and improving emergency response.
